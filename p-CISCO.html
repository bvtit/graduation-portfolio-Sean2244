Description

I completed the entire first CCNA course with merit. The course consisted of 11 chapters, each with a specific topic on CISCO and network technologies. In the CCNA course, I had to complete several labs, packet tracers (which are virtual labs that offer more customizability and creativity than physical labs), and end of chapter labs. Fortunately, my shop provides me with the latest router and switch technology to help me practice the subjects physically. I was also given a final project in which I had to create a comprehensive business propasal to a simulated company, which can be seen here. Lastly, I was able to get a 100% on the final exam. Most importantly, this course taught me how to troubleshoot and setup network devices.
