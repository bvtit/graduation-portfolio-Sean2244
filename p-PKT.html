Description

This project was my CCNA Routing and Switching: Introduction to Networks, final project. The project required me to simulate an actual business deal with another company. I created a mock company, invoice, and letter of intent; therefore, the project is formal and compareable to a presentation that is seen in the modern day industry. Additionally, I created detailed descriptions on each device with significant impact network connectivity. Lastly, I created a packet tracer that exactly simuated a working network, in which, every device was able to communicate with eachother on command. I received a 100% on this project and was commended by my instructor.
